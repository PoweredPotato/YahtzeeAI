YahtzeeAI

Welcome to the official website for YahtzeeAI, the neural network dedicated to playing Yahtzee. The purpose of this website is to gather data based on user responses in order to create a dataset to teach an artificial intelligence how to play Yahtzee. Data will be gathered through users like you! Click the link below to go to the second page of the site, where your games of Yahtzee solitaire will be transformed into valuable information that will teach YahtzeeAI how to play Yahtzee! In case you don't know how to play Yahtzee, but still want to contribute, learn how to play by clicking the second link below!
